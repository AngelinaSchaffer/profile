About Me

My name is Angelina Schaffer. I am not your typical graphic designer. While creating designs for clients, I'm also a devoted wife and mother of four daughters.
Balancing work and family can be a juggling act, but I have found a way to manage it all. I'm currently pursuing my education at North Idaho College, and in my
spare time, I dabble in crafting. I have always had a passion for design. I've been drawing and creating since I was a child, and over the years, I have honed in my
skills and developed a keen eye for detail. My clients appreciate my unique perspective and ability to bring their vision to life.
Despite the demands of motherhood and academic pursuits, I still finds time to pursue my hobbies. Crafting allows me to unwind
and tap into my creative side, which helps me recharge and stay focused on my work and studies. Overall, I am a graphic designer, a loving wife, and a
devoted mother of four. I am a woman who wears many hats, but I handle each role with grace and determination
